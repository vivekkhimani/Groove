    About Us

    Spotify and other popular music apps rely heavily on other users data to tailor the best recommendations from millions of options available. Traditionally, they find the music in your playlist and find other users with similar interests and try to recommend music from similar playlists which might not have been a part of your playlist. There might be other instrinsic details in the algorithm, but we are quite sure that this is how they filter the top choices. However, it might not be possible to use such an approach without having access to other users data. Also, using the private data raises privacy concerns.
